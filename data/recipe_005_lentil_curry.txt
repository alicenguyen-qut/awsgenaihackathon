Recipe: Lentil Curry

Category: Main Course
Cuisine: Indian
Difficulty: Medium
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4 people

Description:
A warming and aromatic Indian-style lentil curry made with red lentils, creamy coconut milk, tomatoes, and traditional spices. This vegan and gluten-free dish is rich in plant-based protein and fiber, making it perfect for vegetarians, vegans, and anyone seeking a hearty, nutritious meal. The combination of curry powder, cumin, and fresh ginger creates an authentic Indian flavor profile.

Ingredients:
- 250g red lentils (dry)
- 400ml coconut milk (1 can)
- 1 large onion
- 2 medium tomatoes
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 tablespoon fresh ginger (grated)
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 100g fresh spinach
- 500ml water
- Salt to taste

Cooking Instructions:
1. Rinse the red lentils thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear.
2. Dice the onion finely and mince the garlic and ginger.
3. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
4. Sauté the diced onion for 5-7 minutes until soft and translucent.
5. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, curry powder, and ground cumin. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes until they break down and soften.
7. Add the rinsed lentils and 500ml of water to the pot.
8. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
9. Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until lentils are tender.
10. Stir in the coconut milk and fresh spinach.
11. Cook for an additional 5 minutes until the spinach wilts and the curry is creamy.
12. Season with salt to taste.
13. Serve hot with basmati rice, naan bread, or quinoa.

Ingredient Substitutions:
- Use green or brown lentils (increase cooking time to 35-40 minutes)
- Replace coconut milk with cashew cream for nut-based version
- Add cauliflower or sweet potato for extra vegetables
- Use kale instead of spinach
- Substitute garam masala for curry powder for different flavor
