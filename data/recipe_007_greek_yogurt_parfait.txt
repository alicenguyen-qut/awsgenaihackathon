Recipe: Greek Yogurt Parfait

Category: Breakfast / Snack
Cuisine: Mediterranean
Difficulty: Very Easy
Preparation Time: 5 minutes
Cooking Time: 0 minutes
Total Time: 5 minutes
Servings: 1 person

Description:
A quick and delicious protein-packed parfait layered with creamy Greek yogurt, fresh berries, and crunchy granola. Perfect for breakfast or a post-workout snack that supports muscle recovery.

Ingredients:
- 250g Greek yogurt (plain, non-fat or low-fat)
- 80g mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
- 40g granola
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds
- 10g sliced almonds

Cooking Instructions:
1. In a glass or bowl, add a layer of Greek yogurt (about 1/3).
2. Add a layer of mixed berries.
3. Sprinkle some granola and chia seeds.
4. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
5. Top with sliced almonds and drizzle honey over the top.
6. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 hours.

Nutritional Information (per serving):
- Calories: 380 kcal
- Protein: 28g
- Carbohydrates: 48g
- Fat: 10g
- Fiber: 8g
- Sodium: 120mg

Dietary Information:
This recipe is suitable for: high-protein diets, vegetarian diets, gluten-free (if using gluten-free granola).
Allergens: Dairy, nuts, may contain gluten (depending on granola)
This recipe is NOT suitable for: vegan, dairy-free, nut-free diets.

Health Benefits:
- High in protein for muscle recovery and satiety
- Probiotics from Greek yogurt support gut health
- Antioxidants from berries boost immune system
- Omega-3 fatty acids from chia seeds support heart health
- Sustained energy from complex carbohydrates

Ingredient Substitutions:
- Use coconut yogurt for dairy-free/vegan option
- Replace honey with maple syrup or agave
- Use any seasonal fruits available
- Substitute almonds with walnuts or pumpkin seeds
- Use homemade granola to control sugar content

Meal Timing: Suitable for breakfast, snack, post-workout
Season: All seasons
