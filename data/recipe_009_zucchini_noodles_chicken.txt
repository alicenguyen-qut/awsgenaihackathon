Recipe: Zucchini Noodles with Chicken

Category: Main Course
Cuisine: Italian-inspired
Difficulty: Easy
Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 2 people

Description:
A light and healthy low-carb alternative to pasta, featuring spiralized zucchini noodles topped with lean grilled chicken and a fresh tomato basil sauce. Perfect for weight loss goals while maintaining high protein intake.

Ingredients:
- 300g chicken breast, sliced
- 4 medium zucchinis (about 600g)
- 200g cherry tomatoes, halved
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese (optional)
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Red pepper flakes (optional)

Cooking Instructions:
1. Spiralize zucchinis into noodles using a spiralizer or julienne peeler.
2. Season chicken slices with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
3. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Cook chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
5. In the same skillet, add remaining olive oil and garlic, sauté for 30 seconds.
6. Add cherry tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes until they start to burst.
7. Add zucchini noodles and toss for 2-3 minutes until just tender (don't overcook).
8. Return chicken to the pan, add fresh basil, and toss everything together.
9. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.
10. Serve immediately, topped with parmesan cheese if using.

Nutritional Information (per serving):
- Calories: 340 kcal
- Protein: 35g
- Carbohydrates: 16g
- Fat: 16g
- Fiber: 5g
- Sodium: 380mg

Dietary Information:
This recipe is suitable for: low-carb diets, keto-friendly, gluten-free, high-protein diets, paleo-friendly, weight loss diets.
Allergens: Dairy (if using parmesan - can be omitted)
This recipe is NOT suitable for: vegetarian, vegan diets (unless chicken is substituted).

Health Benefits:
- Very low in calories and carbohydrates
- High in protein for muscle maintenance
- Rich in vitamins A and C from zucchini and tomatoes
- Contains antioxidants from fresh vegetables
- Supports weight loss with high volume, low calorie density
- Heart-healthy fats from olive oil

Ingredient Substitutions:
- Replace chicken with shrimp or tofu
- Use yellow squash instead of zucchini
- Add sun-dried tomatoes for extra flavor
- Use nutritional yeast instead of parmesan for vegan option
- Add spinach or kale for extra nutrients

Meal Timing: Suitable for lunch, dinner
Season: All seasons, especially great in summer
