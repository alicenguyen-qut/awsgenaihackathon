Recipe: Grilled Chicken Salad

Category: Main Course
Cuisine: Mediterranean
Difficulty: Easy
Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 35 minutes
Servings: 2 people

Description:
A healthy Mediterranean-style grilled chicken salad featuring tender grilled chicken breast served over fresh mixed greens with cherry tomatoes and cucumber. This protein-rich meal is perfect for lunch or dinner and supports muscle growth while being low in carbohydrates.

Ingredients:
- 300g chicken breast
- 100g mixed greens (lettuce, arugula, spinach)
- 150g cherry tomatoes
- 1 medium cucumber
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon black pepper

Cooking Instructions:
1. Season the chicken breast generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.
2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
3. Grill the chicken for 8-10 minutes per side until the internal temperature reaches 165°F and the chicken is cooked through.
4. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes to retain its juices, then slice into strips.
5. While the chicken rests, wash and chop the vegetables.
6. In a large bowl, mix the greens, halved cherry tomatoes, and sliced cucumber.
7. Top the salad with the sliced grilled chicken.
8. Drizzle with olive oil and fresh lemon juice before serving.

Nutritional Information (per serving):
- Calories: 380 kcal
- Protein: 42g
- Carbohydrates: 12g
- Fat: 18g
- Fiber: 4g
- Sodium: 450mg

Dietary Information:
This recipe is suitable for: high-protein diets, low-carb diets, gluten-free diets, dairy-free diets, keto-friendly, paleo-friendly.
Allergens: None
This recipe is NOT suitable for: vegetarian, vegan diets.

Health Benefits:
- Supports muscle growth and repair due to high protein content
- Rich in vitamins A and C from fresh vegetables
- Contains heart-healthy monounsaturated fats from olive oil
- Low in carbohydrates, suitable for weight management
- Anti-inflammatory properties from olive oil and fresh vegetables

Ingredient Substitutions:
- Can substitute chicken with turkey breast or grilled shrimp
- Use lime juice instead of lemon juice
- Replace mixed greens with kale or romaine lettuce
- Add avocado for extra healthy fats

Meal Timing: Suitable for lunch, dinner, post-workout meal
Season: All seasons, especially refreshing in summer
