Recipe: Tofu Scramble

Category: Breakfast
Cuisine: American / Vegan
Difficulty: Easy
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 2 people

Description:
A delicious plant-based alternative to scrambled eggs, this tofu scramble is packed with protein and flavor. Seasoned with turmeric for a golden color and nutritional yeast for a savory, cheesy taste.

Ingredients:
- 400g firm tofu, drained and crumbled
- 100g spinach, chopped
- 80g bell peppers, diced
- 60g onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon cumin

Cooking Instructions:
1. Drain tofu and press to remove excess water, then crumble with your hands or a fork.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
3. Add onion and garlic, sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
4. Add bell peppers and cook for another 2 minutes.
5. Add crumbled tofu to the pan, breaking it up further with a spatula.
6. Sprinkle turmeric, nutritional yeast, cumin, salt, and pepper over the tofu.
7. Stir well to coat the tofu evenly with spices.
8. Add spinach and cook until wilted, about 2 minutes.
9. Cook for another 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tofu is heated through and slightly golden.
10. Serve hot with whole grain toast or avocado.

Nutritional Information (per serving):
- Calories: 320 kcal
- Protein: 24g
- Carbohydrates: 18g
- Fat: 18g
- Fiber: 6g
- Sodium: 480mg

Dietary Information:
This recipe is suitable for: vegan, vegetarian, plant-based diets, dairy-free, gluten-free, high-protein diets.
Allergens: Soy
This recipe is NOT suitable for: soy-free diets.

Health Benefits:
- Excellent plant-based protein source
- Rich in iron and calcium from tofu
- Turmeric provides anti-inflammatory benefits
- High in vitamins A and K from spinach
- Low in saturated fat compared to eggs
- Contains all essential amino acids

Ingredient Substitutions:
- Add mushrooms for extra umami flavor
- Use kale instead of spinach
- Add cherry tomatoes for freshness
- Include black beans for extra protein and fiber
- Season with smoked paprika for a smoky flavor

Meal Timing: Suitable for breakfast, brunch, lunch
